Pig (Classic Dice Game)
Rules
1. ROLL DIE : increase this turn's points by the rolled number and continue your turn.
2. HOLD : add this turn's points to your score and end your turn.
3. *Roll a 1* : lose this turn's accumulated points and forfeit your turn.
4. First player to make it to 20 points wins!
